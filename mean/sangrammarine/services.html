Ship Management

SANGRAM MARINE SERVICES provides commercial management service to 3rd party owned vessels mainly by using our professional expertise in commercial management, crew management, technical management, H&M and P&I insurance, general administration and accounting as per standard BIMCO ship management agreement, Our document of compliance for managing 3rd party vessels are issued by Bureau Veritas on behalf of UAE and Panama flag state authority. Ship Management comprising Technical Management and Crew Management is specialty of S M Ship Management. Services are geared towards the efficient operation of all types and all sizes vessels. Vessels are manned, operated and maintained to the highest standards of quality & safety, on behalf of our principals. Ship Management is supported by a comprehensive range of specialist services. We are committed and our actions are firmly oriented towards forming long term partnership with our clients.

TECHNICAL MANAGEMENT

Technical Management encompasses all of the services that maximize the operational efficiency and physical condition of a vessel throughout its working life in compliance with the requirements of industry regulators. Service delivery is underpinned by a rigorous approach to safety, security and environmental protection. Our expert well experience staff utilizes efficient operating systems based on latest technologies and resources available for both shore and ship based operations.

SHIP AGENCY

SANGRAM MARINE SERVICES are experts in handling vessels in the ports of Dubai, Sharjah and Ajman through our head office in Dubai and further handle vessels through our representative offices, sub agents and our associates in the Arabian Gulf and India. Currently handling vessels of PCL Panz New Zealand, Transbulk International Shipping Dubai , Veesham Shipping Dubai, Timber ships Australia, Havi Shipping Dubai to name a few.

SHIP AND CARGO BROKERS

SANGRAM MARINE SERVICES acts as ship and cargo brokers for fixing vessels / cargo within the global cross trade market with various charterers / ship owners / associate brokers worldwide.

CLEARING AND FORWARDING AGENTS

SANGRAM MARINE SERVICES act as clearing and forwarding agents for Farlin timbers, Acme building materials, Al Qawarib wood industries, Dubai and Oman general trading National saw mill, Meshrakh recycling, Dafaf wood Industries, Timber land, Kele contracting, China national building materials.

INDENTING AGENTS

SANGRAM MARINE SERVICES, represents as indenting agents to market / sell products of Pentarch forest products Australia and New Zealand, SRS Ltd New Zealand, Rougier International France, Atlas Timbers Denmark and Mercuria Energy solutions Singapore in the emirates of Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Fujairah and have been highly appreciated and recognized by all our customers.
